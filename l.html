Glass refraction - magnification

Introduction:

Fires caused by glass refraction are uncommon. However, children have been known to experiment with magnifying glasses, which concentrate sunlight into a focal point, generating enough heat to potentially start a fire if the conditions are right.



How to prevent:

Don't focus convex glass lenses on dry objects and supervise children when using magnifying glasses or potentially fire-starting objects.
