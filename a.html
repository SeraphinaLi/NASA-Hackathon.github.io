Arson

Introduction:

Deliberate, malicious fire-setting on property (including public lands) with intent to damage or defraud, often involving the use of devices and "hot sets.”



How to prevent:

Report any suspicious activity in wildfire-prone areas to authorities promptly immediately. Make sure to provide detailed information about any vehicles and individuals present, including dates, times, and exact locations. Having photos and videos can be highly beneficial in aiding investigations.
